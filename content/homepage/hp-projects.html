Games Projects

Bachelor Thesis: “What's the impact of biofeedback in survival horror games?”

In this topic, the development of biofeedback in survival-horror games is analyzed by using the players scare level as a gameplay feature. To do so, testers were recruited to play a custom Unity VR horror experience and their pulse was measured to gather their fear and used dynamically to change gameplay elements.

Aer Racers Demo

AerRacers is a Ghibli inspired multiplayer pod racing game is a racing game demo level developed on the Nintendo Switch, using a custom C++ game engine. In this project, I worked as the Lead Project, Lead Designer and Lead Gameplay Programmer. The demo was developed by 25 team members, 10 Game Arts, 2 Audios and 13 Programmers.

Neko Engine

We've worked on a custom Game Engine named "Neko Engine". The Neko Engine is a 3D game engine based on SDL2 and OpenGL ES 3.0 that works on Desktop (Windows and Linux), WebGL2 and Nintendo Switch, used at the SAE Institute Geneva. Our work consited in creating various tools to improve this custom Game Engine.
